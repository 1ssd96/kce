About Green Key Rentals

Founded in 2015, Green Key Rentals was born from a simple idea: urban living shouldn't come at the expense of the environment. We set out to create apartment communities that combine modern comfort with sustainable practices.

Today, we manage over 50 properties across the city, each designed to minimize environmental impact while maximizing quality of life for our residents.
